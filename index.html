About Me

Hi! My name is Agona and I am an architecture student at NYIT! I am partial to sustainable design and social research. Growing up in NYC, I have a special interest in the cultural and communal protection and aid of local neighborhoods. It is my hope that non-traditional routes in architecture will provide creative solutions for environmentally and socially urgent issues. In my academic projects and extra-curricular ventures, I try to incorporate as many sustainable practices as I can.
